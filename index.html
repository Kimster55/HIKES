Travels across Jutland

One of the most intriguing things about my move to Denmark has been enjoying the abundant nature.

Blokhus Strand

Sand Dunes along the North Sea

There’s hardly a more quintessentially Danish seaside than the wide, white sandy beaches along Jutland’s West coast. Day trippers drive their cars right onto Blokhus Strand and step out onto the windy coast to admire the waves of the North Sea. Sand dunes line the coast and are covered with the robust lyme and marram grass, which help the dunes to survive the strong winds. Pockets of wind shelter between the dunes are perfect for soaking up the sun on a warm Spring day. Read more about Blokhus Strand

Rebild Bakker

Grazing sheep over rolling hills

Rebild Bakker is a large heathland of rolling hills surrounded by forest on three sides. Shrubland heathlands like this one are a rare habitat in Europe. It's a beautiful destination for an easy summer hike. Sheep walk freely throughout the National Park and it is possible to see several traditional thatch roofed farmhouses along the trails.Read more about Rebild Bakker

Ravnkilden

Crisp natural spring

North-West of Rebild Bakker surrounded by woods is a beautiful natural spring. It's a beautiful sight to behold, and the water is cool and refreshing.Read more about Ravnkilde

Store Økssø

Picking blueberries from the forest floor

In late July, the forest floors around Store Økssø lake are covered in delicious blueberry bushes. Walk the trail around the lake and keep your eyes peeled for the fruit-filled bushes. Read more about Store Økssø

Thor Forest

Feeding carrots to the deer

South of the Aarhus city, in the Thor Forest which is part of the larger Marselisborg-Moesgaard forest, lies the Deer Park. Here you can get quite close to the beautiful population of sika and fallow deer. Bring a bag of carrots and you're likely to make some friends! Read more about the Thor Forest

Nordens Kridtgraven

Bird watching at the Norden Chalk Pit

Nordens Kridtgraven is a quarry once used to source chalk and chalk sludge. There are 10-20 metre tall cliffs which look out onto a unusually blue waters. The pit is filled with plants and birds. It's perfect for a morning stroll.Read more about Nordens Kridtgraven

Brabrand Søen

10 bike trip around the Brabrand Lake pathway

The pathway around Brabrand Lake provides a beautiful vista of Midtjyllands diverse landscape. Paved paths all around the lake are the perfect way to see polder, forest of reeds, willow, scrub, fields, and forest areas.Read more about Brabrand Søen

Årslev Engsø

Hidden nature trails

Årslev Engsø is near the city of Aarhus. Yet once you're on the dirt trail, surrounded by nature and abundant bird life, the peace and quiet will make you feel transported far, far away. Read more about Årslev Engsø
